Product Description

This Goodyear 215/65R16 Tire Reduces Fuel Use in All Seasons

The Goodyear Weatherhandler Fuel Max 215/65R16 tire features Goodyear's Fuel Max design to reduce your fuel costs over the life of the tire. The tire's efficient tread compound gives you low rolling resistance, decreasing the amount of fuel you need to go the distance. Goodyear estimates you can save up to 2,600 miles' worth of gas by using these well-designed tires. Goodyear will stand behind these tires with a 65,000-mile limited warranty and a free lifetime replacement promise against manufacturer defects.

The Goodyear 215/65R16 tire is made to deliver great traction, no matter the season. Speed-rated 'T' (118 mph), these tires have an 8/32-inch tread depth and an 'A'-graded traction rating. The tire is designed to last, with a super-high treadwear grade of 620. The tire's cross-section is 215 millimeters, its sidewall height to cross-section ratio is 65 percent, and its wheel diameter is 16 inches.
